## STEP 1: ICLR Submission Requirements

### Paper Format (CRITICAL - Strictly Enforced)

```
LENGTH: Maximum 8 pages (single-spaced, not including references)
FORMAT: PDF via CMT (Conference Management Toolkit)
TEMPLATE: https://iclr.cc/Conferences/2026/StyleGuide
REFERENCES: Unlimited pages

MARGINS: 1 inch all sides
SPACING: Single-spaced
```
