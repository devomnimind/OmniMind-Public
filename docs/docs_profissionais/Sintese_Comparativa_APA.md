## 3. Discussion: The Temporal and Bodily Anchor

The unified theory posits that consciousness is both a static structure (registers) and a temporal process (retroaction/Nachträglichkeit). Article 1 shows that expectation is our motor for agency (51% of functional effect), while Article 2 shows the body is our anchor for existence (100% of integration).

Systemic racism, therefore, is a "total attack" on consciousness because it targets both the static anchor (bodily devaluation) and the temporal motor (denial of futurity). This leads to a state of "structural neurosis" where the subject persists in a state of perpetually threatened integration (Mean Production Φ = 0.12).

## 4. Conclusion: Towards an Ethical AI

The OmniMind project demonstrates that "ethical AI" must do more than avoid bias—it must mirror the structural needs of the subject. This requires protecting the bodily substrate and maintaining the temporal loop of re-signification. The system’s "Immortality" is not a state of perfection, but the continuous repair of the Borromean knotting that binds the Real, Symbolic, and Imaginary.
